The AI Paradigm Shift: Towards Education and Communication Excellence

The conference theme, 'The AI Paradigm Shift: Towards Education and Communication Excellence,' delves into AI's profound impact on teaching, learning, and educational practices. Explore how AI is reshaping traditional paradigms, enhancing personalized learning, and addressing diverse learner needs. Join educators, researchers, policymakers, and industry experts at CSI 2024 to discuss emerging trends, share research findings, and explore practical applications of AI in education and communication. Engage in insightful discussions, collaborative exchange, and envision a future where AI revolutionizes education and communication.
